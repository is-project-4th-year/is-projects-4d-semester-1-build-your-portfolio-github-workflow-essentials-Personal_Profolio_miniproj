About Me

Hi, I'm Tom Ndemo, a Computer Science student and software developer with a strong focus on React, React Native, and Node.js. I'm currently working on impactful projects that range from AI-powered healthcare apps to service discovery platforms like AdInfinite.

I enjoy building practical, user-centered solutions—whether it's a lung sound classifier using CNN-BLSTM or a tourism app to help international visitors explore Kenya. I’m passionate about clean code, solving real-world problems, and learning new technologies, from machine learning models to scalable APIs.

Beyond tech, I’m deeply motivated by innovation for social good, including disaster relief optimization and empowering local businesses. I work best with purpose-driven goals, and I'm always excited to collaborate and grow.
